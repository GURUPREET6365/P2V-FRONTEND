About Us

P2V (Place to Visit) is a focused travel platform built to make place discovery practical and fast. We highlight useful place details like name, address, pin code, description, and timestamps so users can make quick decisions.

Our goal is to reduce noise and improve clarity for travelers by presenting concise, useful information in a clean interface.

Privacy Policy

We use authentication tokens to keep your session secure and to enable personalized actions like voting. Token data is stored in your browser and sent as a bearer token to authenticated API endpoints.

We do not expose your private account credentials in the frontend. You can log out anytime to remove your session token from local storage.
